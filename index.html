About Me

Master of Data Science Graduate (UWA) | Data and Analytics Engineer

I am a Master of Data Science graduate from the University of Western Australia with a background in Computer Systems Engineering and several years of experience as a full stack engineer. My postgraduate training gave me deep capability across data management, statistics, machine learning, natural language processing, artificial intelligence, optimisation and computational modelling. This combination of skills enables me to work confidently with complex data, design reliable analytical workflows and build models that generate meaningful insights.

I also completed an industry internship in Australia as a data analyst, where I automated reporting processes, developed dashboards and supported operational teams with data driven insights. This experience strengthened my ability to apply analytical thinking in a real business environment and communicate results clearly to non technical stakeholders.

Before moving to Australia I completed my BSc Eng (Hons) in Computer Systems Engineering with first class honours and delivered cross functional software projects for global clients. That work gave me solid skills in building scalable web applications, working in agile teams and collaborating closely with stakeholders.

Now based in Perth I am focused on data engineering, analytics and AI driven solutions. I enjoy taking a problem from requirements and data exploration through to automated pipelines, models and dashboards that support better decisions. I am keen to contribute my mix of software engineering, statistics and machine learning to organisations that want to use data in a practical and impactful way.
